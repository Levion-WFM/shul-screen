Setup notes

• Vercel env vars: RESEND_API_KEY (required) and THANKYOU_FROM (optional, e.g. "BMJ21 <thankyou@bmj21.com>").

• Until you verify a sending domain in Resend, you can only send to the email address that owns the Resend account.

• Browser-side image generation uses Great Vibes script for the name. Drawn at the template's natural resolution so the PNG is full quality.
